About KICK

Welcome to KICK — your ultimate destination for stylish, high-quality sneakers that express who you are. We don’t just sell shoes — we help you walk with confidence, wherever life takes you.

Founded in 2025 by RIEL MANGUBAT, KICK was born from a passion for footwear and individuality. After years of collecting sneakers and following global trends, I decided to launch a space where every shoe tells a story — from streetwear icons to performance legends.

At KICK, we carry brands that lead the industry: Nike, Adidas, Converse, Puma, and New Balance. Every pair in our collection is handpicked to reflect both comfort and character.

We are proudly based in Balingasag, Philippines, and serve sneaker lovers nationwide.
